War & Peace

Leo Tolstoy



$10.85

Considered to be one of the best written novels of all time,
Leo Tolstoy's War and Peace tackles intricate family issues and
struggles for survival during Napoleon's invasion of Russia.
The story follows Pierre Bezukhov, a stout, unhappily married, man who
seeks a purpose and Andrei Bolkonsky, a well established man who is
in search of glory. Despite being published it's age, this book can
connect to many issues faced today. War and Peace is definitely a must read!

Despite being commonly seen as strictly a romance novel,
Pride and Prejudice is also a satire on the time period.
The story follows Elizabeth Bennet, a young woman of little to no
status in society,and Mr.Darcy, a well established and well respected
young man. Jane Austen satirizes the social expectations of marriage
